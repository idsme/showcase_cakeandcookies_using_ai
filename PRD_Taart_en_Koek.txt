# **Product Requirements Document — Taart en Koek Website**

## **1. Project Overview**

**Product Vision:**
Create a modern, responsive, user-friendly website that showcases custom handcrafted cakes, explains the ordering process, displays pricing, answers customer questions, and drives visitors to submit a custom cake quote via WhatsApp or Instagram or follow on Instagram.

**Target Users:**
Local customers in Rotterdam-Nesselande, Zevenhuizen, Cappele a/d Ijssel & Nieuwerkerk seeking handcrafted upper segment custom cakes for celebrations (birthdays, weddings, gender reveals, anniversaries). ([Taart en Koek][1])

**Primary Goals:**

* Clearly communicate services and value proposition
* Simplify quote/order submission
* Promote pricing transparency
* Drive Instagram contact and engagement https://www.instagram.com/taart_en_koek/
* Drive WhatsApp contact and engagement +3164191203

### **1. Custom Order Page**

**Primary Purpose:**
Collect custom cake requests.

**Sections:**

#### **Order Form**

* Fields:

  * Name
  * WhatsApp / Instagram
  * Preferred pickup date & time
  * Cake type (Birthday, Wedding, Gender Reveal, etc.)
  * Size / Portion estimate
  * Flavor choices (Vanilla, Red velvet, Chocolate)
